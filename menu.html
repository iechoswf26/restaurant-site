Our Menu

Step into Four Elements Udon, where every bowl bends flavor like a true master of the elements! Start your journey with Fire Nation Gyoza and perfectly salted Air Nomad Edamame Pods before choosing your destiny: Avatar’s Tonkotsu, light as the wind yet rich as the journey; Zuko’s Fiery Shoyu, bold, smoky, and carrying just a spark of heat; Toph’s Earthy Miso, robust and grounding with unstoppable strength; or the refreshing Water Tribe Vegan, smooth, soothing, and full of life. Each steaming bowl is crafted with balance, power, and heart — just like the legends who inspired them. Pair your feast with tea, boba, or a cool drink of water, and prepare to taste harmony in every slurp.
